Skeptics Silenced! South Campus Finally Discovered!

Last week north campus explorers finally discovered the elusive south campus. Although once thought to be like El Dorado or Atlantis the once believed mythical location has been shown to be real.

On September 8th, 2021 an expedition of north campus explorers found the lost campus at Umass Lowell. Before recently we thought that UML only possessed 2 active campus however the once thought mythical south campus was proven to be real. As many may already know Umass Lowell once posessed a west campus a region of campus that we have already discovered the ruins of. Until this discovery the best scientist of north campus beleved that south must have suffered the same fate or did not exist at all. We at the Riverhawk Tribune asked once south campus skeptic Gregory Kaplowitz what he thought of the recent discovery.

"I certainly never thought this day would come but it appears to be true. Now I'll be the first to admit that I was always one of the most vocal in the scientific community about how this was no more than fantasy and legend but in the face of indisputable proof I'll also be the first to admit that I am wrong.
